Tides Information and Quiz

What Are Tides?

Tides are the periodic rise and fall of sea levels caused by the gravitational forces exerted by the Moon and the Sun, combined with the Earth's rotation. Tides occur due to the difference in gravitational pull exerted by the Moon and Sun on different parts of the Earth.
